About the Playground

The Acropolis Kids’ Playground is a thoughtfully designed space inside Acropolis Park, Victoria Island, built to spark creativity and adventure in children of all ages.

Set against a backdrop of lush greenery and Greco-inspired structures, our playground features:

  • 🛝 Slides, swings, climbing walls & zip-lines
  • 🎨 Creative zones for art, storytelling, and role play
  • ☀️ Safe, padded flooring & shaded rest areas
  • 👮‍♂️ On-site attendants & safety protocols

It's a wonderland where kids can be kids, and parents can relax with peace of mind.
