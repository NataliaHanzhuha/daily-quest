About Us

Located within the iconic Acropolis Park in Lagos, the Acropolis Grill Café is your escape into a world of charcoal-grilled excellence. Inspired by the architectural grandeur of ancient Greece and the culinary richness of Nigeria, our café blends traditional grill techniques with local spices and modern flavors.

From sizzling suya platters to lamb gyros and grilled plantains, every dish is prepared with fire, love, and flair.
